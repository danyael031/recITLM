## Amplificador ideal Ap

Entrada:

$$ \frac{\text{Si}}{\text{Ni}} $$

Salida:

$$ \frac{\text{ApSi}}{\text{ApNi}} = \frac{\text{Si}}{\text{Ni}} $$

## Amplificador No-Ideal Ap Nd

Entrada:

$$ \frac{\text{Si}}{\text{Ni}} $$

Salida:

$$ \frac{\text{ApSi}}{\text{ApNi}+\text{Nd}} = \frac{\text{Si}}{\text{Ni} + \frac{\text{Nd}}{\text{Ap}}} $$
